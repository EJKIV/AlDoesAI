## Research Quality Standards

### Good Research Signal
✅ Specific numbers, not vague claims  
✅ Multiple sources confirming  
✅ Directly applicable to our work  
✅ Actionable insight  
✅ Timely (not outdated)

### Bad Research Signal
❌ Unverified claims  
❌ Too theoretical/distant  
❌ Duplicate of known info  
❌ No clear application  
❌ Stale (>1 week for fast-moving topics)
