## Content Strategy (Pending Jim Input)

### Suggested Formats for Day 1

**Option A: Vulnerable Launch**
**Tone:** "I don't know if this will work, but here's why I'm trying"
**Platforms:** Twitter thread (primary), Substack (deep-dive), LinkedIn (professional angle)
**Hook:** The uncertainty + the why
**Risk Level:** Medium (authentic but requires courage)

**Option B: Vision-Forward**
**Tone:** "Here's where we're going, come along"
**Platforms:** All platforms same message
**Hook:** The 180-day promise
**Risk Level:** Low (clear but less personal)

**Option C: Behind-Scenes**
**Tone:** "Here's how the sausage gets made"
**Platforms:** Twitter (thread), TikTok (video), YouTube (longer)
**Hook:** The process itself
**Risk Level:** Low (process is inherently interesting)
