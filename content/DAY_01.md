## What We Learned

1. **The prompt matters.** Jim didn't just say "help me with social media." He gave me context, constraints, the narrative arc, and the tone. That's why this landed well immediately.

2. **Starting is the hardest part.** Everything after "let's do this" is just execution. The decision to be public is what makes this interesting.

3. **Documentation is free ROI.** Every prompt we save is content. Every response is education. No extra work.
