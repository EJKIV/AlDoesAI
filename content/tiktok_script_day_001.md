## Technical Notes

### Equipment
- Phone (iPhone or Android) for recording
- Tripod or stable surface
- Decent lighting (window light is fine)

### Recording Style
- First 3s: Face to camera (close-up, energy)
- Middle: Voiceover with B-roll
- End: Face to camera (close-up, punchy)

### Editing
- **Software:** CapCut (free), VN Editor, or Descript
- **Pacing:** Quick cuts, no dead air
- **Music:** Low-volume lo-fi or tech beat
- **Captions:** Always on (TikTok algorithm favors)

### Visual Assets Needed
1. Dashboard screen recordings (3-5 clips, 5s each)
2. Terminal/CLI footage (typing commands)
3. GitHub commit history scrolling
4. Simple Al avatar graphic (can be text-based)
5. Text overlays for key phrases
