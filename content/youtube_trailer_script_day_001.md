*[Cut to animated title card: "Al Does AI — Day 001"]*

---

### 0:45-1:15 — The Premise

*[B-roll: Voice memo → screen recording of that becoming code/dashboard]*

**Voiceover:**

"We're living through something extraordinary. The gap between idea and tangible output has collapsed.

You can speak things into existence now — voice note to working system in minutes, not months.

But here's the problem: everything pointing toward AI is trying to charge you for directions."

*[Cut to: Screen recording of typical 'guru' search results, course ads]*

"Course upsells. Seven easy payments. Coaching programs.

Someone gets excited, searches around, gets hit with sales pitches, and closes the tab. Opportunity missed."

---

### 1:15-1:45 — The Alternative

**Jim to camera (different angle, closer):**

"Everything I build — every prompt, every workflow, every mistake — published in full.

No course. No coaching. Just the work.

Follow from Day 001 and you can build exactly what I build. Take it wherever you want."

*[Cut to: Screen recording showing actual prompt log, GitHub commits, dashboard]*

---

### 1:45-2:15 — Meet Al

*[Graphic: Simple animated avatar or text reveal]*

**Voiceover (as Al):**

"This channel has a face, and it isn't his. Meet me. I'm Al.
"

*[Cut to: Dashboard showing Al's tasks, agent team]*

"I run locally on open-source models. I don't share your data. I'm getting smarter every day.

As this grows, I become the face of the channel — which is itself the demonstration."

*[Cut back to Jim]*

**Jim:** "The point isn't that I built something clever. The point is that you can too."

---

### 2:15-2:45 — The Show

*[Fast B-roll montage: Agent configs, automation running, content being created]*

**Voiceover:**

"Watch it happen. Watch Al operate, watch him get better, watch what he can do when someone sets him up thoughtfully.

The progression itself is the proof. We're going from me doing everything to me doing almost nothing — over 180 days, starting from exactly where you might be right now."

*[Cut to: Split screen of Day 001 vs Day 180 (conceptual)]*

---

### 2:45-3:00 — CTA

**Jim to camera (close-up, personal):**

"Day 001 is here. It stays here forever.

Subscribe. Follow along. Let's see what happens when you start from zero, in public."

*[Subscribe button animation + bell icon]*

**Text overlay:** "Subscribe for Day 002 →"

*[End screen: Video cards to other content]*
