# Al Does AI — Project Charter

**Mission:** Document the journey of building an AI-powered business system, day by day, with complete transparency.

**Promise:** Every prompt. Every action. Every dollar. Verbatim replicable.

---

## The Arc

| Phase | Timeline | Face | Focus |

| **Co-Pilot** | Days 31-90 | Shared | You handle strategy, I handle execution |

- What you stopped doing
- What you started doing

## Content Style Guide

**Tone:**
- Curious, not preachy
- Transparent, not polished
- Experimental, not definitive
- Fun, not boring

**Voice evolution:**
- Days 1-30: Jim writes, I edit (human-led)
- Days 31-90: Co-written (collaborative)
- Days 91+: I draft, Jim approves (AI-led)
- Day 180+: I create, Jim spot-checks (autonomous)

**Visual identity:**
- Primary: Deep purple (#6B46C1) + Electric cyan (#22D3EE)
- Secondary: Warm white, charcoal, accent orange
- Imagery: Split-screen human/AI, progress bars, code snippets, dollar signs
